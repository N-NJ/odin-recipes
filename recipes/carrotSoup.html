Carrot Soup Recipe

World's best carrot soup right here

World's best salmon right here

This maple glazed salmon is delicious and very easy to prepare. I love maple syrup in everything and decided to use it in the marinade. My husband totally loved it; he wasn't a salmon fan until now.

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Chicken stock
  • garlic
  • Seasoning
  • butter
  • dill

Steps:

  1. Combine all the ingredients, and bring to a boil
  2. reduce heat and let simmer for 30 mins
  3. puree the soup in the blender
  4. simmer for another 30min
  5. serve warm

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Garlic
  • Maple syrup
  • Seasoning

Steps:

  1. Make the marinade
  2. cut the salmon into fillets
  3. marinate the salmon for 30mins minimum
  4. Bake until fish flakes easily
